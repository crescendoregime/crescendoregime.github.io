Crescendo Regime

Commanding Every Note. Orchestrating Every Life.

The core tenets of the Crescendo Regime are the culmination of Our Fearless Leader's teachings and the foundation on which the Regime exists.

  1. Oceania is the greatest of all nations.
  2. Our Fearless Leader's will defies the petty notion of justice.
  3. Condemnation of the scum of the bucket provides unity of purpose.
  4. Oceania's military is the strongest and mightiest in the world.
  5. The Crescendo Regime is the sole guardian of truth.
  6. The Pitch Police are righteous soldiers that ensure compliance.
  7. Religion is antithetical to perfect and sublime order.
  8. The industry of the Crescendo Regime must be safeguarded at all costs.
  9. Suppression of the proletariat is necessary to ensure future hope.
  10. Music outside of the Regime's score is to be silenced without exception.
  11. The Orchestra Pit is of vital importance to punish dissenters.
  12. The Winch consists of close comrades to prevent unneeded argument.
  13. The Regime has no need for elections and meaningless power struggles.
